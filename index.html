FAQ

Common questions.

What types of leads do you supply?
We supply B2B leads across several formats — exclusive leads to a single buyer, shared leads to a controlled number of buyers, aged batches at reduced cost, and custom-campaign leads built around your audience and geography.
How do you verify your data?
Every contact passes a multi-step validation before delivery: duplicate checks, contact-field validation, consent confirmation, and removal of any record below our quality threshold. What you receive has already cleared internal review.
Can I see a sample before committing?
Yes. We provide a free sample batch before any agreement is signed, so you can evaluate quality, completeness, and relevance firsthand. No minimums and no contracts required to receive a sample.
How quickly can we get started?
We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Once requirements are agreed, first delivery typically happens within 48 hours. Custom-campaign timelines depend on scope and targeting.
What happens if leads are invalid?
We offer replacement guarantees on invalid leads. If a contact doesn't meet the agreed criteria — incorrect data, unreachable, or outside parameters — we replace it. Your account contact manages this directly.
How are leads delivered?
Via CSV, direct CRM integration, or real-time API — whichever fits your existing workflow. We work with your team to match the format to your systems from day one.
