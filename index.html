FAQ

Common questions.

What types of leads do you supply?
We supply B2B leads across several formats — exclusive leads delivered to a single buyer, shared leads distributed to a controlled number of buyers, aged batches at reduced cost, and custom campaign leads built around your specific audience and geography.
How do you verify your data?
Every contact goes through a multi-step validation process before delivery. We check for duplicates, validate contact fields, confirm consent documentation, and remove any records that don't meet our quality threshold. What you receive has already passed our internal review.
Can I see a sample before committing?
Yes. We provide a free sample batch before any agreement is signed. You evaluate the quality, completeness, and relevance of the data firsthand. There are no minimums and no contracts required to receive a sample.
How quickly can we get started?
We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Once requirements are agreed, first delivery typically happens within 48 hours. For custom campaigns, timelines depend on the scope and targeting parameters you provide.
What happens if leads are invalid?
We offer replacement guarantees on invalid leads. If a contact doesn't meet the agreed quality criteria — incorrect data, unreachable, or outside the specified parameters — we replace it. Your account contact manages this directly.
What industries do you cover?
We work across a wide range of B2B industries including financial services, SaaS, insurance, consulting, real estate, and healthcare. We generate leads globally — if your target market is out there, we can reach them.
How are leads delivered?
Leads are delivered via CSV, direct CRM integration, or real-time API — whichever fits your existing workflow. We work with your team to ensure the format matches your systems from day one.
